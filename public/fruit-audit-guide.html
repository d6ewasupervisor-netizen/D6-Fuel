District 1 & 8 Fruit Audit

Field User Guide

Use this guide while taking fruit audit photos. The goal is a complete 360-degree view of every listed fruit set in the store.

Open the Fruit Audit App Open District 1 Signup Dashboard

Before You Start

  1. If you are auditing District 1, claim a store on the live signup dashboard first.
  2. Open the app on your phone, choose your district, and sign in with your approved email.
  3. Select the correct FM store number from the District 1 or District 8 store list.
  4. Allow camera access if your phone asks.
  5. Stay on the same phone until you submit. Photos are saved on that device.

For Each Fruit Set

Each set card shows the commodity, POG, produce table, and bay range. Match that card to the fixture in the store, then tap Start 360 Capture. The camera stays open and walks you through these four photos:

1. Front
2. Right Side
3. Back
4. Left Side

How To Take The Photos

  1. Tap Start 360 Capture on the set card.
  2. Follow the side name shown on the camera screen.
  3. Stand far enough back to show the full fixture side from edge to edge.
  4. Tap the green capture button. The app advances to the next side automatically.
  5. Keep going until Front, Right Side, Back, and Left Side are complete.
  6. If one side is blocked, too wide, or hard to read, add an extra photo for that side.
  7. If you picked the wrong photo, tap the small x on the thumbnail and retake it.

Important Photo Rule

Do not submit until every set shows all four side views captured. The app will keep the Submit button disabled until the store is complete.

Submitting The Store

  1. When all required photos are captured, scroll to the bottom of the store screen.
  2. Add notes if anything is unusual: missing fixture, blocked view, wrong POG, or damage.
  3. Tap the green Submit button one time for the store.
  4. Wait for the confirmation screen before closing the app.

If Signal Is Bad

Your photos stay saved on the phone until they submit successfully. If Submit fails, do not clear browser data. Move to better signal or Wi-Fi and tap Submit again.

Need Help?

Contact the project supervisor before leaving the store if you cannot complete all four sides for a set.
